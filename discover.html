Discover UMD

Why I Chose UMD

When it came time to choose a college, the University of Minnesota Duluth stood out to me for its strong sense of community, beautiful lakeside location, and commitment to hands-on learning. I was drawn to the opportunity to be part of a smaller, more connected campus where I could make meaningful relationships with professors and classmates. UMD's emphasis on real-world experience and its collaborative atmosphere aligned perfectly with my values and academic goals. Coming here was one of the best decisions I've made.

What I Love About Duluth

Duluth is an incredible place with so much character and natural beauty. The Lake Superior waterfront is breathtaking, offering peaceful escapes and outdoor adventures year-round. Beyond the lake, the city has a vibrant downtown with great restaurants, local businesses, and a welcoming community feel. The people here are genuine and supportive, and there's a real sense of pride in the city. Whether it's hiking the Superior hiking trail, exploring the local art scene, or simply enjoying the views, Duluth offers endless opportunities to grow and explore. I've come to love this city and everything it represents.
